A collection of verses
Of Seed & Blossom
✦ I ✦
Poem the First
Seed
A quiet seed beneath the ground,
unknown to sun, untouched, unfound.
It slept beneath the winter sky,
while strangers still kept passing by.

No bloom had reached the air above,
no hint of warmth, no sign of love.
Just silent roots too small to see,
waiting for what could someday be.
✾
✦ II ✦
Poem the Second
Sprout
A fragile stem began to rise,
drawn softly by the morning skies.
With every laugh and passing day,
it leaned a little more your way.

The rain would fall, the winds would roam,
yet still it searched for light and home.
A little green, a little new,
unfolding slowly close to you.
✾
✦ III ✦
Poem the Third
Bud
A tiny bud began to show,
its hidden colors tucked below.
It swayed beneath the silver air,
uncertain if the sun was there.

Yet every moment made it strong,
each quiet glance, each passing song.
And day by day, without a sound,
its roots reached deeper in the ground.
✾
✦ IV ✦
Poem the Fourth
Bloom
The petals opened with the dawn,
soft as the night they once had known.
What started small and pale with fear
now blossomed bright whenever near.

The garden breathed in shades anew,
each blooming thought still led to you.
And like a flower kissed by spring,
my heart began awakening.
✾
✦ V ✦
Poem the Fifth
Full Blossom
Now every field is washed in gold,
where quiet seeds once hid in cold.
The flower sways beneath the light,
no longer scared of open skies.

For day by day its bloom grew true,
fed by the thought and sight of you.
And in its petals, bright above,
there blooms the gentle truth of love.
✾
